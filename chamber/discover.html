Event: Digital Youth MeetUp

The Camber of Commerce of the City of Buenos Aires invites young people to participate of the Digital Youth MeetUp, in which there will be different stands that will provide information and demostration of acceleration, entrepreneurship and incubators. It will take place the 29 and 31 of October! This event is meant for young people from the age of 16 to 35, with the purpose of bringing tools and knowledge to develop and place themselves in the professional and business world.

What do we do?

The 60% of small companies of this city are providers of different services. That is why we think that these companies should have and added value to the development of a modern economy that enhances the competitiveness between small companies.

Our Objectives

  • To be the more direct channel to information of services the chamber of commerce provides to its associates.
  • To make the communication and informatio between secretaries, effective.
  • To train in the most transparent way the information to all questions related to benefits and activities.
