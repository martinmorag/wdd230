Buenos Aires Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Purposes

One of the purposes will be to provide meaningful information regarding the Chamber of Commerce of the Buenos Aires city of Argentina. such as Contact information and their plan of action.

Another one of the purposes is going to be to to provide what are going to be the next strategies to promote the commerce in the city of Buenos Aires.

Scenarios: some questions that will be answered

What are the events that will occur regarding the Chamber of Commerce?

What is the contact information?

What do they do and what are their objectives?
