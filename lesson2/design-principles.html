Proximity

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

Church of Jesus Christ

I believe this website reflects the principle of proximity because it shows all of the things of the same kind in the same place, for example, the tools are all together in the same place and the libraries are next to each other.

Alignment

Facebook

Facebook

Facebook is an excellent example of alignment, because when you enter in your profile, you see the feed on the center of the website, the chats and events on the right and your setting on the left.

Repetition

BYU Pathway Worlwide

BYU Pathway

In this example we see a lot of repetition, which is good because it is in harmony altogether, the color yellow is repeated and the fonts as well.
